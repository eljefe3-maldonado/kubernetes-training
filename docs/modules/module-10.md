# Module 10: Incident Response And Forensics

## Why This Matters

Kubernetes incidents move quickly and are easy to make worse through rushed
containment. Response discipline matters as much as technical depth.

## You Will Learn

- triage and evidence preservation
- containment without premature destruction
- credential rotation and persistence review

## Key Questions

- What should be isolated first?
- What evidence is most likely to disappear?
- How do you recover without reintroducing the same risk?
