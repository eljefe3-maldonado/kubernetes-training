# Module 06: Admission Control And Policy-As-Code

## Why This Matters

Admission is one of the strongest places to stop unsafe workloads before they
become runtime problems.

## You Will Learn

- preventive policy design
- audit-first rollout strategy
- exception handling without weakening standards

## Key Questions

- Which controls belong in admission rather than CI or documentation?
- How do you move from audit to enforce safely?
- What does a justified exception actually look like?
